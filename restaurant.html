The Brief

This project was made to improve on a local restaurants website. A restaurant page is the perfect place to change the order of information. Typically contact info is at the bottom of sites, but people who go to visit this site usually are looking for location and time first.

The Approach

I wanted to create a page that shows the information hierarchy displayed differently than a lot of sites. This is a rapid prototype for the restaurant to see the layout.

The Result

The site keeps the most important information up top, however, it does not take much to get to the menu. The images of food and the information about the different menu items come next. This can almost serve as a digital menu for while customers are choosing their plates off the train as it passes.
