The Brief

This app is a type of scanvanger hunt guide. The client wanted something that could take the user through a guided tour and collect badges along the way.

The Approach

My approach starts with sketches and lo-fi builds. In this case I am strictly on the design side. I am team lead and working with the client and developers to create this app. We moved from sketches to wireframes to static mockups. We used Invision to help build out interactive prototypes from our different iterations of prototypes.

The Result

The finished product (mainly dealing with the UI) went in a little different direction than what you see here. The app was built with ease of the user in mind. There is a main map page which allows the user to see where they are located in relation to all of the points of the hunt.
