Restaurant Site

This project was made to improve on a local restaurants website. I wanted to create a page that shows the information hierarchy displayed differently than a lot of sites. A restaurant page is the perfect place to change the order of information. Typically contact info is at the bottom of sites, but people who go to visit this site usually are looking for location and time first.

Quick Thoughts

The thing I love most about design & development is all of the different approaches you can take. Which angle is best when it comes to one problem or another?
