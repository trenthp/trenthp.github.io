The Brief

This project is intended to make the construction process much easier for everyone involved. Currently there is a lot of financial risk trying to get everything done on time and on budget.

The Approach

I started with business analysis to make sure that when we started the design and development process we would have everything laid out in a concise order. Working with the client we worked through the real-world construction process and began working through ideas that would allow the current paper process to be something that works for on screen. I made sure to have user interviews with general contractors to collect information from different sets of users.

The Result

There is much more to when you can see in the images, however, do to the project still being under development I am not able to so more than what you see. The current iteration of the app includes custom dashboards for all users (everyone from the general contractor to the banker to the home owner), risk management, financial management, team communication, and everything in-between.
